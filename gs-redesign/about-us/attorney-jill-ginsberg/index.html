Senior Partner / Attorney

Jill Ginsberg

A board-certified specialist whose work centers on protecting South Florida's elderly and incapacitated from financial exploitation and abuse.

Jill is Board Certified by the Florida Bar in Elder Law and brings both legal and financial fluency to guardianship, estate planning, probate, and trust administration.

Before private practice, Jill worked in first-tier accounting firms including Deloitte and KPMG as director of personal financial planning practices. At Ginsberg Shulman, she has focused much of her practice on protecting vulnerable adults and helping families through incapacity and guardianship matters.

What Jill Handles

  • Guardianship and incapacity proceedings
  • Financial exploitation and abuse of vulnerable adults
  • Estate planning and asset transfers
  • Probate matters and trust administration
  • Tax controversy and planning
  • Asset protection and charitable planning

Jill works from downtown Fort Lauderdale and has lived in Hollywood for more than two decades.
