Managing Partner / Attorney

David Shulman

A board-certified wills, trusts, and estates attorney with a tax background from the IRS Office of Chief Counsel.

David's practice is dedicated solely to estate planning, including tax planning and asset protection, and probate and trust administration.

After graduating from Brandeis University and George Washington University Law School, David joined the Internal Revenue Service as an attorney-adviser in the Office of Chief Counsel, Passthroughs and Special Industries, writing regulations, revenue rulings, and private letter rulings for partnerships, S corporations, and trusts.

Why Estate Planning

David returned home to Broward County after seven years with the IRS and earned an LL.M. in Estate Planning from the University of Miami. He later became Board Certified by the Florida Bar in Wills, Trusts and Estates.

How David Practices

For estate planning, David prepares the core planning documents himself. Staff may assist with administration, court filings, and workflow, but the legal strategy and final work product remain attorney-led.

Outside the Office

David serves on the board of the Jewish Community Foundation of the Jewish Federation of Broward County and has a weakness for long-distance train travel.
